Urban Akwu's Clientele

Livestock Farmers (Poultry and Aquaculture farmers)

We provide all Ranges of organic poultry and fish feed as well as trainings and extension programs for livestock farmers

Pet Fish / Aquarium owners

Who only keep very small scale, do not often require same forms of feed needed by medium to large scale fish farmers, we offer a range of organic pet sized and planned fish feed. For pet fish owners.
